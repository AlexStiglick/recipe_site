High Protein Breakfast for 4:
Kolbassa, Egg & Cheese Tortilla

Ingredients (for 4 People):

  • Kolbassa Sausage — 200g total (50g per person) — 12g protein | 160 cal pp | $1.40 total
  • Eggs — 4 (1 per person) — 6g protein | 75 cal pp | $1.50 total
  • Casa Mendosa Tortillas — 4 (1 per person) — 6g protein | 210 cal pp | $1.40 total
  • Cheese — 80g total (20g per person) — 6g protein | 100 cal pp | $1.00 total

Instructions (10 Minutes):

  1. Prep Kolbassa: Slice 200g sausage into coins. Sear 2-3 mins until crispy.
  2. Cook Eggs: Crack 4 eggs, season, scramble/fry 3 mins.
  3. Warm Tortillas: Heat in pan or microwave 20-30 secs.
  4. Assemble Wraps: Egg + Kolbassa + Cheese. Optional: Sear for crispy finish.

Nutrition Breakdown Per Person:

Protein: ~30g

Calories: ~545 cal

Cost: ~$1.83

Total Cost for 4 People: $5.30
